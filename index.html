Makgolane Mbongwe Inc

Makgolane Mbongwe Inc is a youth owned Attorneys & Conveyancers firm in corporate law registered with the South African Legal Practice Council under practice number F67652.
Our law firm was established on the 04th of June 2021 by excellent and ambitions youth in the names of Mr Mohube Kholofelo Makgolane with an aim to provide quality legal services to companies, individuals and state institutions.

About the firm

We possess a vast experience stemming from the private and public sectors and are well equipped to handle any legal problem with maximum diligence, skill and excellence. We are committed to devoting the same attention to all our client’s legal matters and doing so with the utmost professionalism, care and dedication.
We are looking forward to growing our practice into viable law firm that employs young, skilled and dedicated energetic people in the Republic of South Africa, thus maintaining profitability and sustainability.
Our client base is not only limited to individual clients, we are capacitated to provide quality legal services to companies, mining sector, municipalities, state institutions and non-governmental organisations.
